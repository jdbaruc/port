Native Kontrol Standard Tooling

Once I found out how the partnership coordination team was handling and ingesting assets from our partners, I knew something had to be done. I created a shareable Figma file which each partner would then duplicate and upload their artwork. By ingesting the 3 key assets -- artwork, GUI screenshot, and logotype -- the file spits out the 10 assets required in our SDK all the while offering a preview of each realization of their graphics in our products.
