Quickstart Guides

Native Instrument’s products can be quite sophisticated. Having a rewarding experience out of the box helps the user up the learning curve. All our products can be fun and exciting to learn quickly but my design colleague and I felt that exactly “how” our products could give that “aha” moment early on, was not covered in the existing learning content and resources out there. We wanted to create a common format which users could rely on to get that “aha” moment as soon as they open their new product. I outlined and created these in collaboration with the Learning Team.
